About me


I am Dominic Chigozie

A versatile web developer with experience in building engaging digital environments. I've developed my expertise over the years using a variety of web development tools and technologies, always aiming to create responsive and user-friendly websites.

I'm proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I have an affinity for designing engaging online interfaces. I ensure sure that my work is responsive and provides the best user experience by employing frameworks like Bootstrap and React. With practical WordPress experience, I've built, updated, and redesigned a number of websites, ensuring that the material is always current and the navigation is always clear. With a foundation in PHP and a working knowledge of Python and Java, I can build effective server-side functionalities. I have experience maintaining data security and integrity when working with databases like MySQL and SQLite. I'm passionate about both graphics and UI/UX design in addition to coding. I believe in the union of form and function, making sure each website not only functions perfectly but also looks good.
